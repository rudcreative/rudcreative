The Creator

Why
Rud
Creative.

I'm David Rudnicki, a self-taught videographer and editor whose journey began on the road. At 18, I spent weeks traveling across the United States, living out of a car with friends, chasing experiences, and documenting everything with a camera.

What started as a passion project quickly turned into something bigger. Through my first three YouTube films, I generated over 100,000 views on long-form storytelling content — and discovered the power of creating videos that people genuinely connect with.

Since then I've expanded into short-form content, producing promotional videos for brands, businesses, athletes, and creators. My work has generated 200,000+ views across Instagram and TikTok. Every business has a story worth telling.
